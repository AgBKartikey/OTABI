Installation guide:

Clean Flash:

- Back up all your data

- Wipe Cache,Dalvik,Syste,Data,Vendor

- Flash last A10 Firmware for your region

- Flash Rom

- Flash DFE if you don't want to be encrypted, otherwise you need the format data

- reboot system



Dirty Flash

- Back up all your data

- Flash Rom

- Flash DFE

- Wipe Cache

- reboot system
